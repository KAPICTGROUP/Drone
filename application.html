Applications

Drones are used in agriculture (crop monitoring), delivery (e.g., Amazon Prime Air), search & rescue, and environmental science (wildlife tracking).

  • Science: Mapping ecosystems or monitoring pollution.
  • ICT: Data transmission via 5G and AI for autonomous operations.
