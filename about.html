What is a Drone?

A drone, or unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV), is an aircraft without a human pilot on board. It can be controlled remotely or autonomously using software.

Key components include propellers, batteries, cameras, and sensors. Drones are used in photography, delivery, and surveillance.
