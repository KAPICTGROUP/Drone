About Drones

Drones originated from military technology in the early 20th century, with the first unmanned aircraft used in World War I. Modern drones combine sensors, GPS, and AI for autonomous flight.

  • History: From 1917's Aerial Target to today's consumer quadcopters.
  • Types: Fixed-wing, rotary-wing, and hybrid drones.
