What is a Drone?

A drone, also known as an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV), is an aircraft that operates without a human pilot onboard. Drones are controlled remotely or can fly autonomously using software and sensors.

Types of Drones

Quadcopters

Most common type, used for photography and recreation.

Fixed-Wing Drones

Long flight time, used in mapping and surveillance.

Military Drones

Used for defense, surveillance, and combat missions.

Applications of Drones

  • Aerial photography and videography
  • Agriculture and crop monitoring
  • Delivery services
  • Disaster management
  • Security and surveillance

Future of Drones

The future of drones includes autonomous delivery systems, smart cities, medical supply transport, and advanced AI-controlled flight systems.
